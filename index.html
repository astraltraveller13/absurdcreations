Sounds From
The Edge

Vale is the solo project of Berlin-based producer and sound artist Mara Solano. Drawing from the fringes of electronic music, her compositions dissolve the boundaries between silence and sound — between the analog and the infinite.

Her 2024 debut record Hollow Light was described as "a meditation you didn't know you needed" — recorded in an abandoned textile warehouse over three solitary months, the album pulses with restrained intensity.

Vale performs live with a custom modular rig and a single spotlight. Nothing more.
